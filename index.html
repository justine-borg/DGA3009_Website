ABOUT THE ARTIST


Justine Borg

Graphic Designer and Photographer driven by the love for art.

Justine Borg is based in the beautiful island of Gozo, Malta.

Studied at Sir M.A Refalo, Victoria all the way up to the University of Malta.

Narrowed my studies with a Bachelor Degree of Fine Arts in Digital Arts.

Passionate about capturing life through the lense, I find many artist as inspirations.

The list includes photographers like Robert Mapplethorpe, Lissa Rivera, Lucy Hilmer, Dita Pepe, Nan Golding and more
